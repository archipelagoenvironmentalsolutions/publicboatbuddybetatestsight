Boat Buddy Beta Test Information

For Testers

Introduction

Welcome to the Boat Buddy Beta Test! We appreciate your participation and feedback.

How to Begin

  1. Download the Boat Buddy app from the given link.
  2. Install the app on your device.
  3. Open the app and register using the provided beta tester credentials.

Providing Feedback

Once you have tested the app, please provide your feedback through the in-app feedback form or via the provided email address.

Important Notes

  • The beta version may contain bugs. Please report any issues you encounter.
  • Do not share the beta version or your tester credentials with others.
  • Your feedback is crucial for improving the final version of Boat Buddy.

Thank you for being a part of the Boat Buddy Beta Test!
