About Boat Buddy

Boat Buddy: Your Ultimate Marine Provisioning Assistant

Embarking on a cruise should be smooth sailing, and with Boat Buddy, provisioning becomes one less thing to worry about. Whether you're planning a short trip or an extended voyage, our app ensures that you're well-equipped for the journey.

Set sail with Boat Buddy! Organize, locate & share provisions effortlessly. Discover nearby supplies & connect with fellow sailors. Your ultimate maritime mate awaits.

Key Features:

  • Provisioning Planner: Allocate the essentials for each cruise, ensuring you're always prepared.
  • Visual Organizational Screen: At a glance, view and manage your provisions and ship stores with ease.
  • MapKit & CoreLocation Integration: Find the nearest provisions in real-time. Whether it's a last-minute necessity or a rare item, we've got you covered.
  • Social Provisions Sharing: Got extra supplies? Share them with your boat neighbours or find others who might have what you need.
  • Boat Buddy Community: Connect with fellow sailors, share experiences, and build a maritime community that supports and uplifts.

Ready to streamline your provisioning process and connect with a vibrant boating community? Dive into Boat Buddy and make every voyage a memorable one!
